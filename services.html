Services

Gargoyles Statuary has been a unique gift and home & garden décor resource in the U-District for 17 years. A variety of mythological and historical statuary can be found as well as a unique assortment of silver and pewter jewelry and hand made treasures. We offer a 10% discount to U-PASS members on statuary, jewelry, T-shirts, and candles.

We have an in-store Art Show every 3rd Friday as part of the U-District Art Walk where we feature a local artist and throw a reception. We also offer tarot and astrology readings.

Need a gargoyle? Gargoyles are useful as:

  1. Bird baths
  2. Wind chimes
  3. Pen holders
  4. Paperweights
  5. Bookends
